Privacy Policy

App: Pluviora
Developer: Jacobo Technologies
Last updated: February 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Pluviora ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Pluviora requests access to your device's GPS location solely to automatically detect your geographic coordinates. This information is used only to provide local rainfall and weather data within the app.

We do not collect, store, or transmit your location data to any external server or third party.

2. How We Use the Information

Location data is processed entirely on your device in real time. It is used exclusively to:

  • Calculate local rainwater harvesting estimates
  • Provide location-relevant results within the calculator

3. Third-Party Services

Pluviora does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

4. Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data. No information is stored on our servers because we do not operate any servers that collect user data.

5. Children's Privacy

Pluviora is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
